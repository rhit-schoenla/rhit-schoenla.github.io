About Me

Hello! I am a mechanical engineer and artist in my free time. While you might be wondering how an artist is also an engineer, the combination of technical perspective and creativity are perfect for both portraiture and mechanical design.

I graduated college at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology in 2025 and highschool at Dreyfoos School of the Arts in 2021. I started painting pets in 2018, after teaching art classes and private lessons. I prefer to work in acrylic paint, but also enjoy watercolor and digital media.

Please check out my portfolio of recent works and click on the order page to see information about getting your own pet(s) painted. Pet portraits can be the perfect gift!....or perfect wall decor for your own home!
