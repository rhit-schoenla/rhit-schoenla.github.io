About Me

Hello! I am a current student at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology studying Mechanical Engineering. While you might be wondering how an artist is also interested in entering a STEM field, the combination of technical perspective and creativity are perfect for both portraiture and mehcanical design.

I am a recent graduate of Dreyfoos School of the Arts as of May 2021 and started painting pets in 2018, after teaching art classes and private lessons. Recently, I have won two Scholastics Art and Writing Awards: an Honorable Mention in 2020 and a Silver Key in 2021. I prefer to work in acrylic paint, but have dabbled in oils and digital media.

Please check out my portfolio of recent works and click on the order page to see information about getting your own pet(s) painted. Pet portraits can be the perfect gift!....or perfect wall decor for your own home!
